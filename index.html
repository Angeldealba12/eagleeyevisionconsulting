Tara Louisa Allen

CEO & FOUNDER

Tara Louisa Allen is a dynamic leader, visionary entrepreneur, and passionate advocate for personal and professional growth. A former United States Air Force Weather Meteorologist and holder of an MBA in Strategic Leadership, Tara draws from a diverse background spanning the military, government work, auditing and law, early childhood learning brain sciences, marketing, higher education, and entrepreneurship. This multifaceted experience allows her to bring a unique and innovative perspective to every endeavor.

As the founder of Eagle Eye Vision Consulting, Tara harnesses this wealth of experience to inspire and empower individuals and organizations to unlock their full potential. Guided by the mantra "Live in Color," her mission is to create healthier, more inclusive communities and vibrant, effective workplaces.

Mission

At Eagle Eye Vision Consulting, we empower individuals and organizations to unlock their unique strengths and potential. Through transformative workshops and purpose-driven strategies, we inspire innovation, adaptability, and self-discovery. Our goal is to foster vibrant, inclusive cultures that prioritize mental well-being, resilience, and meaningful connections, enabling communities to thrive through collaboration and shared purpose.

Vision

Our vision is a world where clarity of purpose and the celebration of individual talents fuel progress and collective impact. By cultivating understanding, leadership, and a deep sense of belonging, we aim to build communities that thrive on collaboration, inclusivity, and sustainable growth for a brighter future.
